Chrome / Firefox / Samsung Internet
  1. Tap on the 3 vertical dots to open the options menu in your browser.
  2. Select "Add to Home Screen" from the option list and follow the prompts.
Other Info
  • Ensure that your browser is in "Regular Mode" and not in "Private Browsing Mode"
  • For other browsers, follow similar steps as above and select "Add to Home Screen" to install the app on your phone.

Safari Browser
  1. Click the share button in your browser.
  2. Scroll down (if needed) and select the "Add to Home Screen" Button
